About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Regional Economics at the Shanghai University of Finance and Economics (since Sep 2025). My research focuses on the Digital Economy and Regional Economics, as well as Health Economics. I have published over a dozen papers in international peer-reviewed journals including Economic Analysis and Policy, Urban Climate, Kybernetes, and others.

Prior to my doctoral studies, I obtained my Master's degree in International Trade from the University of Science and Technology Beijing and my Bachelor's degree in Economics from China Pharmaceutical University.
